2. Snap-Joint Edge Profiles — SBX-WALL-165

How it works: Male tongue (30×25 mm) slots into female groove (31×26 mm, 1 mm clearance per side). EPDM 6 mm Ø cord seated in tongue-tip groove compresses ~17% against groove bottom on assembly — achieving passive airtight seal without adhesive. Shoulder faces provide the structural bearing surface.

6. CNC Machining Notes

Tool Setup

— Router bit: solid carbide straight 2-flute, Ø 6 mm for EPDM groove; Ø 12 mm or 16 mm for main shoulder profile
— Router speed: 18,000–24,000 rpm; feed rate 2–4 m/min through PUR/OSB
— Profile depth: single pass if PUR core is pre-set; multiple passes (5 mm steps) through OSB
— Climb-cut on final pass to minimize OSB tear-out
— Vacuum fixture strongly recommended (panel warps under single-side clamping)
— Dust extraction required (PUR foam produces fine particulate; N95 minimum)

DXF File Usage

— Files sbx-wall-165-male.dxf and sbx-wall-165-female.dxf contain 2D edge profiles in DXF R12 format
— Coordinate system: X = 0–165 mm across panel thickness; Y = 0 at face datum, negative = into material
— PROFILE layer: closed polyline of the machined edge shape
— DIMS layer: reference dimension annotations (not tool paths)
— Import into CAM software (Fusion 360, VCarve, Mastercam) → generate 2.5D contour tool path
— Panel length (2800 mm) is the third axis; set Z ramp at panel ends to avoid corner chip-out

Quality Check

— Dry-fit every 10th panel pair: tongue should slide into groove by hand with ≤ 20 N force
— EPDM cord should compress visibly but panel faces must be flush (≤ 0.5 mm step)
— Airtightness: apply smoke-stick test at joint; no visible leakage
— OSB surface at tongue/groove edge must be clean-cut; sand any feathering with 80 grit
— Mark each panel face (M/F) and panel number before shipping

Assembly Sequence

1. Place Panel A (female groove) on foundation, plumb and brace
2. Insert EPDM cord into tongue groove of Panel B (male) — press to seat, do not glue
3. Align tongue to groove opening; engage by pressing perpendicular to panel face
4. Drive 4 × Ø6×120 screws at 45° through shoulder into Panel A at 600 mm pitch
5. Check alignment; proceed to next panel
6. Full wall assembly completes airtight envelope — no tape, no sealant required
7. At corners: dedicated corner panel (SBX-CORNER-165, separate drawing)
