## 1. OVERVIEW

We require **12 units of 20-foot ISO shipping container conversions** for a two-story martial arts retreat compound in rural Hokkaido. The containers will be arranged in a U-shape (open south), stacked 2 stories high — 6 containers on the ground floor, 6 containers directly stacked on top. Total structure height: ~5.2m. Climate: −30°C winter minimum.

**Configuration:**
- Ground floor (6 units): Bedroom A, Bedroom B, Kitchen/Dining, Bathroom/Wellness, Utility/Storage × 2
- Upper floor (6 units): stacked directly on ground floor units — Bedroom C, Bedroom D, Open Room × 2, Lounge, Storage
- North wall: 4 containers (2 wide × 2 high)
- East wall: 4 containers (2 long × 2 high)
- West wall: 4 containers (2 long × 2 high)

**Important:** We want **shell + insulation + windows/doors only**. No interior fit-out. Interior finished locally in Japan.

These containers will be stacked 2-high with human occupancy in the upper floor. The following additional requirements apply:

| Item | Specification |
|---|---|
| Corner casting reinforcement | 6mm steel doubler plate welded around all 8 corner castings (top and bottom) |
| Stacking load rating | Upper floor containers must certify ≥ 192 tonnes stacking load (standard ISO) — provide mill certs confirming |
| Twist lock provision | Bottom of upper-floor containers: 4× ISO twist lock sockets pre-welded for securing to lower container top corners |
| Anti-racking welds | 4× 50×50×4mm steel angle braces welded diagonally at each end of container, both upper and lower units |
| Structural engineer sign-off | Factory must provide structural calculation report confirming 2-high stack with 2.0 kN/m² live load on upper floor |
